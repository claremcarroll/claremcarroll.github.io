About

VRB is a social VR platform centered around user expression and novel methods of communication. Our user-facing applications include VRB Home (a social city), and VRBFoto (a spherical photo sharing app). These work alongside our service toolkit-- an extensible avatar creator, headset-to-mobile broadcasting tool, and persistent backend system-- to help users create virtual worlds that are shareable, friendly, and meaningful.
